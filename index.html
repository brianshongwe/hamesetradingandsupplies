Welcome to Hamese Trading and Supply

Hamese Trading and Supplies is a leading service provider in the construction industry specializing in partitioning, flooring, barricading, ceiling, carpentry and aluminum work in general. We aim to enhance our work by providing the best quality services to our clients. We are proud to supply and fit quality products through our broad network with quality manufacturers in our company.

VISION

To be a one stop shop for all interior design work that includes partitioning, ceiling, flooring, barricading, aluminum doors and windows, electrical, plumbing, carpentry and world-class furniture in order to ensure vast growth of our business by creating job opportunities for our community.
