Our Story

Hamese Trading and Supplies is a leading service provider in the construction industry specializing in partitioning, flooring, barricading, ceiling, carpentry and aluminum work in general. We aim to enhance our work by providing the best quality services to our clients. We are proud to supply and fit quality products through our broad network with quality manufacturers in our company.

QUALITY POLICY STATEMENT

To provide on-going skills training and development so Hamese Trading and Supplies can deliver premium value on all the projects carried out on behalf of our clients. Enviromental Management Plan: We undertake to cause no unnessary harm or damage to the environment, instead develop an appreciation within our organization and the surrounding community to maintain and cultivate the areas in which we work. Health and Safety Plan: Health and Safety are vital to ensuring the completion on time of all projects undertaken. Therefore, Management will adhere and enforce all existing Health and Safety regulations in force at this time.

VISION

To be a one stop shop for all interior design work that includes partitioning, ceiling, flooring, barricading, aluminum doors and windows, electrical, plumbing, carpentry and world-class furniture in order to ensure vast growth of our business by creating job opportunities for our community.

VALUES

Hamese Trading and Supplies is committed to quality, integrity, and innovation in construction. We prioritise customer satisfaction, safety, and sustainability while delivering exceptional workmanship and reliable solutions.

MISSION

To be the first choice service provider in the Interior design work. We aim at creating a well established institution that not only sustains relevance and excellent service in the Republic of South Africa but also resonates with the African continent as a whole.
